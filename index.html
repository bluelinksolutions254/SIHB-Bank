About SHIB BANK

SHIB BANK (Smart Holding Investment Bank) is an innovative financial technology platform designed to transform how individuals, businesses, investors, suppliers, and service providers send, receive, manage, and secure money transactions.

Our platform combines secure digital payments, escrow services, trade finance, business financing, payroll solutions, and international transaction capabilities into one powerful ecosystem. We are committed to making financial services accessible, transparent, efficient, and secure for everyone.

Through advanced technology and customer-focused solutions, SHIB BANK is creating a trusted financial environment where opportunities grow, businesses thrive, and transactions happen with confidence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about SHIB BANK

What is SHIB BANK?

SHIB BANK (Smart Holding Investment Bank) is an innovative financial technology platform that provides secure digital payments, escrow services, trade finance, business financing, and payroll solutions for individuals and businesses.

How secure are transactions on SHIB BANK?

We employ advanced security systems, fraud protection mechanisms, and escrow accounts to ensure every transaction is safe and secure. All customer data and funds are protected with bank-grade encryption and security protocols.

What services does SHIB BANK offer?

We offer digital payments, escrow services, trade finance (LC, BG, SBLC, DLC, PoF, PoP), business loans, payroll management, real estate payments, investment services, and consumer financing for devices and more.

Can I use SHIB BANK for international transactions?

Yes! SHIB BANK supports cross-border transactions and trade finance in over 10 countries including China, India, Turkey, UAE, UK, and USA. We provide full international payment and trade support.

How do I get started with SHIB BANK?

You can get started by clicking the "Open Account" button on our website or downloading the SHIB BANK app from Google Play or the App Store. The onboarding process is quick and simple.

Does SHIB BANK offer business financing?

Yes, we provide flexible business loans and credit facilities up to KES 5,000,000 for SMEs and enterprises, along with trade finance solutions to help businesses grow and expand globally.
